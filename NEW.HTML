Concrete Batching Plants are basically divided into three major types based on quantity of concrete to be produced per hour, namely Less than 30cu.m/hour, 30 to 60 cu.m/hour, More than 60 cu.m/hour. We at SCHWING Stetter India provide all types of batching plants suitable for RMC, Infrastructure, Roads, Rural and Metro applications. Every batching plant can be customized to your specific requirements. For example, ready mix plants can be configured with IoT, multiple cement silos with individual silo weighing systems, weigh bridges etc, whereas for infrastructure sector our customers prefer additional accessories like ice and silica weighers in addition to the standard scope of supply. Whatever be your requirement, get in touch with us to assist you in finding the perfect concrete batching plant for you.
